Our Vision

Glorify God and become a leader of excellence in church architecture.

Our vision statement defines our direction, our purpose and the heart of who we are.

In fulfilling this vision, we will work together to:
  • Train a dedicated team of church design professionals.
  • Engage in strong strategic partnerships.
  • Expand our regional reputation in church design.
  • Establish a national reputation for excellence in church design.
  • Capture, cast and transform a client’s vision into reality.
Our Mission

Our mission statement defines how we corporately plan to fulfill our vision: Consistently exceed our client’s expectations by providing architectural solutions of excellence, superior service and maximizing of ministry potential.

Founded in 1991, Barnes Design Group, P.C. is a leading-edge design firm with a combined 120 years of experience among our staff of talented architects.

We are a firm with a mission.

We are dedicated to serving our clients with an uncommon degree of passion and vision, and we are bound together in our commitment to provide the highest quality design for a variety of clientele.

Barnes Design Group has been awarded in excess of 700 project commissions, and has established a unique niche in the architectural community: specialized architectural design and master planning services for the local church with over 500 church commissions.
