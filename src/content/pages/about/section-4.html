Our Core Values

The principles that guide our practice

Our “Core Values” represent the things that we value most both individually and as an organization.

God-Centered

We believe we must operate according to Biblical principles.

01
Accountability

We believe that personal accountability is foundational for success.

02
Excellence

We believe that excellence is required by God and inspires and motivates others.

03
Prayer

We believe that prayer releases the power of God and clarifies vision.

04
Integrity

We believe that integrity is essential for establishing trust-based relationships.

05
People

We believe that people matter most to God and should matter most to our organization.

06
Vision

We believe that we are called to assist our clients with clarifying and fulfilling their vision.

07
Creativity

We believe that creativity is a gift and a foundational in creating innovative solutions.

08
Partnerships

We believe architecture is a cooperative effort and strategic partnerships are important.

09
Teamwork

We believe that teamwork occurs best in an environment of trust and cooperation.

10
Legacy

We believe we are created, gifted and called for a higher purpose.

11
Significance

We believe we are to assist clients to realize their potential adding value to their lives.

12
—

A continued commitment to growth, learning, and purposeful impact.
